Pasta Carbonara

Description

This bacon carbonara is a super rich, classic "bacon and egg" spaghetti dish that's great to serve for company. This recipe also makes an unusual brunch offering.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Cook meat
  3. Add eggs
  4. Mix
  5. Add cheese
